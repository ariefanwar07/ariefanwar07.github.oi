CC8 - Big Data: Extracting a Story from Millions of Prices

This section visualizes insights derived from the UK Price Data.

Chart 1: Average Monthly Price of Basmati Rice Over Time

Description: This chart shows the trend of average monthly basmati rice prices over time using the `db_prices` dataset.

What I Did:

  • Dataset Source: I obtained the UK price dataset (`db_prices.csv`) based on monthly data from the Long Run Price Database (Davies, 2021) in this link.
  • Data Extraction: I filtered the dataset for rows corresponding to basmati rice (item_id = 210203).
  • Data Cleaning: I converted the `quote_date` column to a datetime format and handled missing values by dropping incomplete rows.
  • Data Aggregation: I calculated the average monthly price of basmati rice using a group-by operation.
  • Visualization: Using Altair, I created a line chart to visualize the average monthly price trend.

Chart 2: Regional Price Comparison for Basmati Rice (January 2022)

Description: This chart compares the average price of basmati rice across regions for January 2022.

What I Did:

  • Dataset Sources:
    • Price Data: I obtained the UK price dataset (`db_prices.csv`) based on monthly data from the Long Run Price Database (Davies, 2021) in this link.
    • Region Metadata: I obtained the region mapping dataset (`db_region.csv`) based on monthly data from the Long Run Price Database (Davies, 2021) in this link.
  • Data Filtering: I selected data for January 2022 and filtered rows corresponding to basmati rice (item_id = 210203).
  • Region Mapping: I merged the dataset with `db_region.csv` to map region codes to their corresponding names.
  • Data Aggregation: I calculated the average price of basmati rice for each region in January 2022 using a group-by operation.
  • Visualization: Using Altair, I created a bar chart to compare the average basmati rice prices across regions.
