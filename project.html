Introduction

Exploring the Impact of Trade on Indonesia’s Economic Stability

Project Overview:

The objective is to analyze the relationship between Indonesia’s trade and key economic indicators such as GDP, inflation, and foreign reserves. The study aims to uncover trends, identify key drivers of trade, and assess their influence on economic stability.

Data Sources:

  • UN Comtrade
  • World Bank
  • IMF

Chart 1: Indonesia Trade Data Visualization

This project visualizes Indonesia's trade indicators from 1990 to 2020, including exports, imports, and trade balance, using World Bank data. The chart highlights trends over time, offering insights into economic stability.

Data Source: World Bank API
