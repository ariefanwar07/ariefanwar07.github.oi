Introduction

Urban population refers to people living in urban areas as defined by national statistical offices. Explosive growth of cities globally signifies the demographic transition from rural to urban, and is associated with shifts from an agriculture-based economy to mass industry, technology, and service. In principle, cities offer a more favorable setting for the resolution of social and environmental problems than rural areas. Cities generate jobs and income, and deliver education, health care, and other services. Cities also present opportunities for social mobilization and women's empowerment.
